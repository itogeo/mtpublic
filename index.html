What is the Score?

Each opportunity is scored 0–100 based on four factors:

Gap (0–40 pts) — How close is the road easement to the public land? Buffer overlap = 40 pts. Smaller gaps score higher.

Land Type (0–20 pts) — BLM & State Trust score highest (often landlocked). USFS and FWP score lower (often already accessible).

Parcel Size (0–20 pts) — Larger parcels = more land unlocked by a single access point.

Isolation (0–20 pts) — Bonus for parcels likely to be landlocked (BLM/State = 15, FWP = 10, USFS = 5).
